Help & support

Find quick answers to common questions about registrations, tickets, and networking. If you’re still stuck, send us a message and we’ll get back to you.

Frequently asked questions

How do I change the name on my ticket? ›

Go to My Tickets, select the event, and choose Update attendee details. Some events lock updates 48 hours before they begin.

I didn’t receive my confirmation email—what should I do? ›

First, check your spam or junk folder. If it’s not there, use the same email address to sign in and view your tickets. You can always download a PDF ticket directly from the portal.

Can I get a refund or transfer my ticket? ›

Refunds and transfers depend on the event organiser’s policy. On the event details page, look for the Refund policy section. If transfers are allowed, you’ll see a Transfer ticket button next to your registration.

How do online / hybrid events work on the day? ›

For online and hybrid events, we email a secure join link 24 hours before the event. The same link is pinned to your My Tickets page under the event name.

Is my payment and data secure? ›

Payments are processed via a PCI-DSS compliant provider. We store only the information needed to manage your registration and never share your details with third parties without consent.
