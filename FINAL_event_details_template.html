EDU Innovate Conference

29 Jan 2026 Canberra, AU Format: On-site

About this event

EDU Innovate brings together educators, learning designers, and technologists to explore how digital tools can support inclusive and engaging teaching. Across one full day, you’ll hear practical case studies, short talks, and panel discussions from schools, universities, and industry.

Who is this for?

This event is suitable for:

  • Secondary and tertiary educators experimenting with blended or online teaching.
  • Learning designers supporting course development and assessment redesign.
  • Education leaders planning digital strategy, analytics, or AI use policies.

What you’ll learn

  • How institutions are trialling AI tools in ways that support, not replace, educators.
  • Design patterns for low-stakes online practice, feedback, and formative assessment.
  • Simple ways to use analytics to understand student engagement and risk.

Tracks & topics

AI in the classroom Assessment design Learning analytics Accessibility Online labs
